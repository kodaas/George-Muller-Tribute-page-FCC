Müller's

Timeline

1999 - 2015

At the age of 70, George Müller decided to devote the next period of his life to a world-wide ministry of preaching and teaching, sharing his Christian faith. Leaving the orphan homes under the direction of his daughter Lydia and her husband, James Wright, he spent the next 17 years travelling, during which he toured the United States of America four times, India twice and, on two further occasions, toured Australia and the Colonies. In addition, George Müller preached in 42 countries, including China and Japan.

Müller's

Quotes

The vigor of our spiritual life will be in exact proportion to the place held by the Bible in our life and thoughts. The first great and primary business to which I ought to attend every day is to have my soul happy in the Lord. Money is really worth no more than as it can be used to accomplish the Lord's work. Life is worth as much as it is spent for the Lord's service. Faith does not operate in the realm of the possible. There is no glory for God in that which is humanly possible. Faith begins where man's power ends. Intimate knowledge of God is possible if we habitually search His Holy Scriptures & translate what we find into obedience. No one ought to expect to see much good resulting from his labors in word, and doctrine, if he is not much given to prayer and meditation.

- - George Müller
